Zurich Insurance North America

Working with Goettsch Partners and St. Louis based developer Clayco, the new campus headquarters for Zurich Insurance North America was the largest project I contributed to while working at Hoerr Schaudt. I was involved at every stage of the project, from schematic design through late construction documents. It was my first experience with corperate campus design, and thoroughly introduced me to design development and construction level drawing process.
