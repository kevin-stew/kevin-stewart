Raised Cedar Beds


Raised vegetable beds are a simple DIY backyard project in theory, but in order to achieve an intentional effect , one that looks orthogonal and generally aligned, a fair amount of planning must go in to such a project. Seeing an opportunity to make use of an under-used space between clotheslines, and realizing there was a hard to perceive slope in the lawn, I used 3D scanning to inventory my site, plan dimensionally realistic planters in a Sketch Up model, and anticipate the nuances of slight grade change as they would impact construction.



Additionally I was able to scan an example of one of the uninstalled beds. A small file size was traded for crispness of rectilinear resolution.


Beyond modeling, the only thing left to do was purchase an astounding amount of garden soil and invest many hours of manual labor on the weekends. Although five beds were originally planned, only three were able to be installed this season.
