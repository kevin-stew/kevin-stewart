Streamside Residence

Although relatively small, this residential project had no shortage of complexities due to its unique situation between a local river, valley-wide irrigation ditch, and state highway. The site plan review process through the county yielded a complex puzzle for deciphering what was, and was not, allowable based on environmental setback requirements. Despite county code hinderances, a landscape design was realized to complement the modern architectural design, featuring a marble slab decorative entry sculpture, recessed auto court, and lower-level terrace with custom spa design.
